SEDAN

No matter the journey ahead, the Polo Sedan is prepared to navigate anything. From its engine to its smart technological features, it's all you need to go the distance

The Polo Sedan offers a reliable 1.6-litre MPI engine that delivers 81kW of power, confidently taking you from point A to B. It also improves traction and stabilises movement through curves, guaranteeing a smooth journey where you're in complete control.

The Polo Sedan embraces Volkswagen technology that manages to feel both recognisable and innovative. These include a multi-function dashboard display, wireless App-Connect, and wireless charging. In addition, the Polo Sedan features a brand new infotainment system called the MIB Regio Radio. This 10-inch radio features improved graphics and a revised layout. It also enables the creation of different user profiles with the ability to lock and unlock each profle using a 4-digit PIN. Its ease of use and responsiveness enhances the user experience.
